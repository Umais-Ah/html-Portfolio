My top three movies of all the times

Up

This is my favorite animated movie. I love the theme song of this movie so much. This movie gives me nostalgic vibes and a sad feeling at the same time.

The Shawshank Redemption

An amazing thriller and prison movie; I love the acting. One of the first Hollywood movies I watched.

Into the Wild

An adventure movie based on a true story. This movie gave me an escape from the real world.
